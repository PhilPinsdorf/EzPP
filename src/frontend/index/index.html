EzPP

Why?

Elevate your Party to the next Level with Easy Party Playlist! After being on many partys I noticed, that everyone wants to hear their favorite song, but they can't because they have not the Controll over the Music.

With this tool everyone can add Songs to your Spotify Queue without giving them access to your phone. They can scan a QR-Code you printed out and add Song via a Web-Interface.

Instructions

  1. The Host logs in here with his Spotify account to get access to this service. (Attention: A Spotify Premium Account is neccessary!)

  2. In the Dashboard you can activate and deactivate this service, copy the link for your friends, generate a new link or print out a QR-Code to scan for your friends.

  3. Hang the sheet with the QR-Code on a wall where it can be seen. Your friends can scan this Code to add Songs to your queue via a web-interface.
